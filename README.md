Smart Resume Analyzer & Career Guidance System, a live, web-based application available online that analyzes resumes and provides structured career insights across Engineering, Commerce, Arts, Creative, IT, Cybersecurity, and Management domains.

This platform is designed to simulate modern ATS-style resume evaluation while offering career guidance based on real-world skill and domain alignment.

Users can upload their resume online through the web interface.

The system applies NLP-based text extraction to analyze resume content.

It identifies skills, projects, internships, and academic/domain relevance.

An ATS-style scoring mechanism evaluates resume strength and completeness.

Based on the analysis, the system generates:

Suitable job role recommendations

Average salary insights (India & international markets)

High-demand cities and industries

Training requirements and recommended courses

A downloadable career report (PDF) is generated for the user.
